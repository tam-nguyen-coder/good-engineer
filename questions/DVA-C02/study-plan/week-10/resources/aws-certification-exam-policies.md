## 🎯 Điểm thi quan trọng (tóm tắt tiếng Việt)
- **Đặt lịch phải qua AWS Certification Account** (aws.training/Certification, dùng AWS Builder ID) → chọn **Schedule with Pearson VUE** → chọn địa điểm/ngày/giờ hoặc online proctored.
- **Reschedule/cancel:** được **dời lịch tối đa 2 lần**; muốn dời lần 3 phải huỷ rồi đặt lại. **Huỷ/dời phải làm ≥ 24 giờ trước giờ thi** → hoàn phí đầy đủ. **Trong vòng 24h** không huỷ/dời được và **mất toàn bộ phí** (trừ khi có giấy tờ ốm/khẩn cấp → xin excused absence với Pearson VUE).
- **Online proctored:** thi tại phòng riêng, dùng máy của bạn, có giám thị qua **webcam + chia sẻ màn hình**; **English 24x7**. Bắt buộc **giao tiếp với giám thị** để hoàn tất. Không được rời khỏi khung hình camera (kể cả đi vệ sinh) suốt bài thi.
- **Giấy tờ tuỳ thân:** cần **ID chính phủ hợp lệ**, tên khớp với đăng ký; yêu cầu ID **khác nhau** giữa online và test center — đọc kỹ trước. Nếu không có ID nước sở tại (trừ EU) → cần **hộ chiếu quốc tế** làm ID chính + 1 ID phụ.
- **ESL +30:** người không phải bản ngữ tiếng Anh xin **thêm 30 phút** khi thi bằng tiếng Anh — chỉ cần **xin 1 lần**, áp dụng cho mọi kỳ thi sau. Chọn trong Account → Request Exam Accommodations → **ESL +30 MINUTES**.
- **Accommodation cho người khuyết tật:** phải **xin trước khi đặt lịch mỗi kỳ thi**, phối hợp với Pearson VUE (đặt lịch qua điện thoại).
- **Comfort aids** (thuốc, thiết bị y tế…) không cần duyệt trước nhưng danh mục cho phép khác nhau theo nhà cung cấp — kiểm tra trang comfort aids của Pearson VUE.
- **Thanh toán:** thẻ tín dụng (Visa, Amex, Mastercard, JCB) hoặc **voucher**. Giá **Associate = $150 USD**. Lưu ý **voucher không gia hạn được** ngày hết hạn.
- **Dùng AWS Exam Demo** của Pearson VUE để làm quen giao diện (đánh dấu câu review lại, đổi màu tương phản, toggle tiếng Anh cho đề dịch).

## 📄 Nội dung (trích từ tài liệu gốc)

# AWS Certification Before Testing

## Eligibility

All AWS Certifications may be earned without completing specific prerequisites. AWS provides recommendations for knowledge and experience aligned to the content and level of proficiency for each certification.

### Recommended Knowledge and Experience
AWS recommends hands-on experience covering competencies, domains, and objectives in each exam's content outline. Multiple training courses, whitepapers, and blogs are available to support preparation. AWS maintains impartiality and does not require or endorse specific courses.

### Teen Testing Policy
Candidates ages 13-17 may take AWS Certification exams with parental or legal guardian consent.

### Nondiscrimination
AWS endorses the principle of equal opportunity and nondiscrimination in all certification programs.

## Exam Overview and Pricing

### Exam Overview
Exams sample knowledge required to perform tasks in major responsibility domains, defined through job task analysis (JTA). Exam guides contain content outlines including domains, objectives, and question percentage distributions.

### Exam Pricing Policies
- 100% vouchers remain valid for full exam price despite exchange rate changes
- 50% vouchers cover 50% off the exam price at purchase time
- Exams may be rescheduled twice only
- Cancellation more than 24 hours before appointment results in full refund of paid exam fee

### Beta Exams
AWS Certification uses beta exams to validate performance of exam questions before use on standard versions.

**Same as standard versions:** valid assessments; passing earns the certification (active three years); scored against minimum proficiency standards; results within 5 business days.

**Different from standard versions:** contain more total and more scored questions; available for limited periods (generally 1–3 months); offered at a discount; can be attempted once only.

## Accommodations

### Accommodations Commitment
Reasonable accommodations are provided to individuals with disabilities to ensure equitable access.

### Requesting Accommodations
Reasonable accommodations are granted for applicants with documented disabilities in coordination with the test delivery provider. **Accommodations must be requested prior to scheduling each exam.**

### English as a Second Language (ESL +30)
A 30-minute exam extension ("ESL +30") is available upon request to non-native English speakers taking exams in English. Request once only; applies to all future exam registrations.

**To Request ESL +30:**
1. Sign in to aws.training/Certification
2. Select **Go to your Account**
3. Select **Request Exam Accommodations**, then **Request Accommodation**
4. In the Accommodation Type dropdown, select **ESL +30 MINUTES**
5. Select **Create**

### Accommodations at Pearson VUE
Candidates requesting Pearson VUE-granted accommodations must contact Pearson VUE directly; approved recipients receive phone-based scheduling instructions.

### Comfort Aids
Specific comfort aids (medicines, medical devices, other items) do not require prior approval and may be brought to test centers. Allowable comfort aids differ by test delivery provider.

## Scheduling Exams and Testing Options

### Creating an AWS Certification Account
To schedule a certification exam you must first create an AWS Certification Account (distinct from AWS accounts), via aws.training/Certification using an AWS Builder ID.

### Testing Options
AWS Certification exams are currently offered through Pearson VUE.

### AWS Exam Demo and Functionality
Use the AWS Exam Demo tool to become familiar with the interface: color contrast options, flagging questions to review later, and the English-language toggle for translated exams.

### Online Proctored Exams
Online proctoring is available for all AWS Certification exams through Pearson VUE.

**Proctoring Languages and Availability:**
- English: 24x7
- Japanese: Monday–Saturday, 9 a.m.–4 p.m. JST
- Spanish (Latin America): Monday–Friday, 10:00 a.m.–5:45 p.m. EST
- Mandarin (Mainland China): Monday–Friday, 8:00 a.m.–5:00 p.m. CST

Communication with a proctor is required to complete an online-proctored appointment.

### Scheduling
1. Sign in to aws.training/Certification
2. Select **Go to your Account**, then **Schedule New Exam**
3. Find the exam; select **Schedule with Pearson VUE**
4. On the Pearson VUE dashboard select location, date, and time; purchase the exam; receive a confirmation email
5. Review the confirmation email for policies and terms

### Rescheduling or Cancelling
1. Sign in to aws.training/Certification → **Go to your Account** → **Manage Pearson VUE Exams**
2. On the Pearson VUE dashboard select the exam from **Upcoming Appointments**

**Rescheduling Rules:**
- Reschedule up to **24 hours** before the scheduled exam time
- Each exam appointment can only be **rescheduled twice**; a third change requires cancellation and new scheduling
- Verify voucher expiration before modifying; **voucher expiration dates cannot be extended**

**Within 24 Hours of Exam:**
- Cannot cancel or reschedule; full delivery fee charged, no refund
- With documentation for illness/emergency, the provider may waive the fee and allow rescheduling — contact Pearson VUE to request an excused absence

### Cancellation Policy
Cancel a minimum of **24 hours** prior to the appointment. Cancelling less than 24 hours before, or failing to appear, forfeits the exam fee.

### Identification (ID) Requirements
A valid government-issued ID is required; different requirements apply for online vs in-person testing. Without a qualifying government-issued ID from the testing country (except EU ID holders), an international travel passport is required as the primary form, plus a secondary form.

### Payment Methods
Pay using a credit card or voucher. Accepted credit cards at Pearson VUE: Visa, American Express, Mastercard, and JCB.

### Test Delivery Provider Terms and Conditions
When scheduling with Pearson VUE you must agree to the provider's terms and conditions — read them carefully before scheduling.
