# Câu hỏi #56

What are the two primary ways a Scrum Master keeps a Developers working at its highest level of productivity? (choose the best two answers)

## Options

**A.** By ensuring the meetings start and end at the proper time.
**B.** By removing impediments that hinder the Developers.
**C.** By facilitating Developers decisions.
**D.** By keeping high value features high in the Product Backlog.
