# Câu hỏi #58

You are the Scrum Master of a new, to be developed product. Development is going to require 45 people. What is a good first question-for you to suggest the group thinks about when forming into teams? (choose the best answer)

## Options

**A.** How will we make sure all teams have the right amount or expertise?
**B.** What is the right mixture of senior and junior people on each team?
**C.** Who are going to be the team leads?
**D.** Who are the subject matter experts on each team?
