# Câu hỏi #181

Management need to monitor progress on a daily basis and decide to do so by attending the Daily Scrum. What are the likely results? (choose three)

## Options

**A.** Management will be able to better direct the work of the Developers.
**B.** Additional facilitation may be required to keep to the time-box.
**C.** Developers may end up with less time in the Daily Scrum to re-plan their work.
**D.** Developers may be less open and transparent during the Daily Scrum.
