# Câu hỏi #214

A new Developer joins the Scrum Team taking the total number to 11 people. As a Scrum Master, What should you do?

## Options

**A.** Instruct the Developers to split into 2 teams.
**B.** Do nothing, the Developers must fix its own issues.
**C.** Instruct the Developers to split into 2 teams, they should decide the appropriate sizes.
**D.** Raise the increased team size as a potential issue and help the Developers decide what to do about it.
