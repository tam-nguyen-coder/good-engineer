# Câu hỏi #208

Who can do the work to ensure the Product Backlog is refined to a state that it is useful?

## Options

**A.** The Business Analyst.
**B.** Stakeholders.
**C.** The Developers, with support from the Product Owner who is still accountable for it.
**D.** The Product Owner.
